**Sponte Study** It's a free, open-source, and 100% offline application. It was built to bring a new way to learn new things, with new and interesting features such as: voice reading of notes, syntax highlighting for code, and other ideas to come.
Sponte Study prioritizes privacy and the needs of the community, trying to be an open-source application with a minimally modern and attractive interface for new users, in addition to aiming to help people study and learn more easily every day, whether they are developers, school students, technical courses, exams, or something else..
